That's my story

Once upon a time...

I'm joking! I'm Bryan, a Junior UX designer based in Italy üçï. Ever since I was a kid, I have always loved IT and design.
I believe that in IT there always is a solution. And I live my life with this attitude: is there a problem? I analyze it, go to stakoverflow and ... Oh NONO! I fix it.
There is inherent problem-solving in my being.
My education? That's a bit strange. Since I was 9 years old, I have constantly tried to make something out of my first PC - my dream was to become an "IT technician".

A dream instantly interrupted by the choice of the middle-school. I decided in fact to study accounting. I undertook this journey both for my analytical and problem-solving skills, and for my parents suggestions.
After 5 years (6 to be honest) I've graduated in accounting and I started to work in the tourism field as a receptionist. Two years later, I was a hotel receptionist, an accountant, an IT assistant, and in my free-time I was studying Economics at the University.

During the Covid-19 pandemic, I have become aware that the life I was living did not really belong to me. Furthermore I realized that I was living the nightmare of my teen ages.

All of a sudden, my dream, the one I had since I was a kid, got back in my life. I started to surf in the net and I discovered an impressive amount of brand-new innovative careers. And among the many possibilities, one jumped out at me - the Start2impact online course. And here I am today, with a professional profile in the Ux design field. From day one I have loved this stunning new world. A world that fits my being, ever since I was young.

And yes, I am finally ready to live my dream job with you.
